Our story

Built by people who care for seniors every day.

HomeTogether didn't come from a tech company. It came from a caregiving one.

HomeTogether was created by the team behind Caring Companions, a family‑owned senior‑care company. Every day, our caregivers help older adults live safely, comfortably, and with dignity in the place they love most — home.

And every day, we saw the same quiet heartache: distance. Grandchildren growing up in photos instead of in person. A daughter three states away, wishing she could just see her mom's face and know she was okay. Wonderful families, kept apart by miles and by technology that was never built for the people who need it most.

Smartphones and tablets ask a lot of an older adult — accounts, passwords, tiny buttons, updates. But there's one screen almost every senior already knows and trusts: the television. So we built HomeTogether to turn that familiar screen into an open door — for a face, a voice, a wave goodnight.

What we believe

Connection is care. Staying socially connected is one of the most powerful things for an older adult's health and happiness — as real as any medication.

Technology should disappear. The best tool is the one your loved one never has to think about. HomeTogether just works, quietly, in the background of the life they already live.

Families deserve peace of mind. Knowing your mom or dad is okay today shouldn't require a dozen phone calls. A gentle check‑in should be enough.

The Caring Companions connection

Because HomeTogether grew out of hands‑on caregiving, it works beautifully alongside it. Families who have virtual visits or in‑home care with Caring Companions get HomeTogether at half price — $75/month — a caregiver for the hands‑on moments, and HomeTogether keeping everyone close the rest of the day.
