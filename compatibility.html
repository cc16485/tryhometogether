Will it work with their TV?

Almost certainly yes. Let's make sure.

HomeTogether is built to fit the TV your loved one already has. Answer three quick questions and we'll confirm, no smartphone or new TV required.

1

Does their TV have an HDMI port?

Nearly every TV made since about 2009 has one. Look on the back or side for a slot labeled HDMI, the same kind of port a streaming stick or cable box uses.

2

Is there Wi-Fi or internet at their home?

HomeTogether connects over the internet. Home Wi-Fi is perfect. No internet at all? You're still covered, keep reading.

3

Do they use a cable or satellite box?

Either way is fine. If they do, it plugs into HomeTogether and their normal channels keep working exactly as before.
