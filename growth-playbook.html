01

Referral outreach Free · highest ROI

Discharge planners, senior centers, and skilled-nursing facilities send home-care agencies a steady stream of clients — because they need somewhere safe to send people going home. You already have a 270-organization contact list built. Work it in this order.

Who to contact first

Start here
Hospital discharge planners & case managers

CoxHealth & Mercy (Springfield), Citizens Memorial (Bolivar), Lake Regional, Ozarks Community. Highest volume — people going home who need help now.

Start here
Skilled nursing & rehab facilities

Ask for the social worker / discharge coordinator. Residents going home after rehab are ideal clients.

Start here
Senior centers & SeniorAge case managers

They talk to families who are just realizing they need help. Warm, local, and free to reach.

Next
Assisted living communities

For residents who need supplemental one-on-one care the community doesn't provide.

Next
Hospice & home-health agencies

You provide the non-medical hours between their skilled visits. Complementary, not competitive.

Next
Elder-law attorneys & financial planners

Their clients are doing Medicaid & long-term-care planning. You help with HCBS applications.

Your pitch in one line: “We're a local, family-owned agency whose caregivers are screened, insured W-2 employees — and we can usually start care the same or next day, including nights and weekends.” Fast starts + reliability is exactly what a discharge planner needs.

Google Ads & Local Services Paid · fastest volume

Two Google products, in priority order. Start with Local Services Ads — they're pay-per-lead and sit above everything.

A. Local Services Ads (“Google Guaranteed”)

  • These show at the very top of search with your rating and a green check. You pay per lead (a call or message), not per click.
  • Set up at ads.google.com/local-services-ads. You'll verify your license/registration, insurance, and pass background checks — which also builds trust.
  • Set your service area (your counties), your services, and a weekly budget. Start modest (e.g. a few leads/week), then raise it as calls convert.
  • Dispute junk leads — Google credits wrong-number or out-of-area leads if you report them promptly.
  • Answer fast. LSA rewards businesses that pick up; missed calls hurt your ranking and waste spend.

B. Search campaign — structure

Point every ad to a fast, single-purpose page with a phone number and short form. Good targets you already have: /getting-started or /book. Add a call extension so mobile users tap to call.

05

Facebook & Instagram ads Paid

Google reaches people already searching. Facebook reaches the adult children who haven't searched yet but are worrying about Mom or Dad. Your buyer is usually a daughter, 45–65, near you.

Audience

  • Location: Springfield + a 25–40 mile radius (or target your 7 counties). This keeps spend on real prospects.
  • Age: 45–70. Gender: all, but expect most leads from women (daughters are the primary decision-makers).
  • Detailed targeting (optional): interests like Caregiver, Aging, AARP, Alzheimer's Association, Elderly care. Facebook may show these as broad — that's fine; the location + age does most of the work.
  • Objective: Leads (an instant form: name + phone) or Calls. Both keep the prospect on Facebook and are cheaper than sending them to the site.

Three ads to test

Ad 1 · The worry (safety)
Primary text: Worried about Mom or Dad living alone? You're not alone — and you don't have to figure it out by yourself. Caring Companions provides trusted, in-home help across Southwest Missouri: companionship, personal care, meals, medication reminders, and more. Screened, insured caregivers. Care can start this week. Headline: Keep Your Parent Safe at Home Button: Learn More / Get a free consultation

From a few hours to overnight. You deserve support too. Headline: A Break for Family Caregivers Button: Get Started
Ad 3 · Aging in place (hope)

Caring Companions is a local, family-owned agency helping Southwest Missouri families do exactly that — and we'll help you check for Medicaid and VA benefits that may cover it. Headline: There's Another Way to Age Button: See If You Qualify

Creative direction

  • Use warm, real photos — a caregiver and senior smiling together, not stock-clinical images. A short 10–15 sec video of Samantha talking to camera outperforms almost anything.
  • Keep your logo and phone number on the image. Add a caption line: “Serving Springfield & SW Missouri · (417) 234-8494.”
  • Budget: $10–$20/day per ad for the first week. Turn off the losers, put the budget on the winner.
  • Always follow up a lead-form lead within 5 minutes if you can — speed is the whole game.
