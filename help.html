Quick‑start setup

1

Plug the device into your TV

Connect it to any open HDMI port on the TV — the same kind of slot a streaming stick uses. Note which one (HDMI 1, HDMI 2, etc.).

2

Switch the TV to that input

Using the TV remote, press Input or Source and choose the HDMI port you plugged into. You'll see the HomeTogether welcome screen.

3

Connect to Wi‑Fi

Follow the on‑screen prompt to pick your home Wi‑Fi and enter the password once. That's the last setup step your loved one will ever need.

4

Add family in the app

Download the HomeTogether companion app on your phone to add contacts, schedule calls, and set reminders. Then make your first call!

Troubleshooting

Common questions

The TV isn't showing HomeTogether +
Make sure the TV's input is set to the HDMI port the device is plugged into — press Input or Source on the TV remote and cycle to the right HDMI (1, 2, 3…). Also check that the device's power light is on.
It won't connect to Wi‑Fi +
Double‑check the Wi‑Fi password (it's case‑sensitive) and that the router is nearby and working. If your network has a 2.4GHz and 5GHz option, either works. Still stuck? Call us and we'll get you connected.
I can't see or hear on a call +
Make sure the TV volume is up and not muted, and that both people have a strong internet connection. Ending and restarting the call usually clears it right up.
How do I add another family member? +
Open the companion app on your phone, go to Contacts, and send an invite. Once they accept, they can call the TV too — and join group calls.
How do I schedule a call or a reminder? +
In the companion app, choose Schedule to set up a call time, or Reminders to add gentle on‑screen nudges for medications, meals, or appointments.
Can I cancel or return it? +
Yes. You have a 30‑day risk‑free trial — if it's not right for your family, contact us for a full refund, no restocking fees. After that, you can cancel the monthly plan anytime.
How does billing work? +
HomeTogether is $149/month (or $75/month for Caring Companions families), billed to your card with free shipping. You can update payment or cancel anytime — just reach out.
