Questions families ask

Does my parent need a smartphone or computer? +
No. That's the whole point. Your loved one only needs their TV. You manage everything — contacts, scheduling, settings — from the companion app on your own phone.
How hard is it to set up? +
It plugs into an HDMI port on the TV (like a streaming stick) and connects to Wi‑Fi with a simple one‑time setup. We include step‑by‑step instructions and a support team to help. After setup, it's automatic for the senior.
Can the whole family be on a call? +
Yes — group calls let grandkids, siblings, and caregivers all join at once, right on the big screen.
Is it private and secure? +
Yes. Calls are encrypted, and only the family and caregivers you approve can ever connect to the device.
What if my loved one can't work a remote? +
Calls from approved family can answer automatically, so all your loved one has to do is look at the screen and say hello.
What does it cost, and can I cancel? +
HomeTogether is $149/month, with free shipping and a 30‑day risk‑free trial — cancel anytime. And if your family also uses Caring Companions for virtual visits or in‑home caregiving, your plan is half off at just $75/month.
What do I need at home for it to work? +
Just a TV with an HDMI port and a broadband Wi‑Fi connection. That's it.
