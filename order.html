Tell us about your loved one.

This sets up the optional virtual caregiver add‑on for your HomeTogether. A few details, who it's for, who to keep close in an emergency, and, our favorite part, a little about them, so a caregiver's very first visit already feels like time with a friend.

Takes about 5 minutes. Requires a HomeTogether device, and you can add this now or anytime later. Everything you share stays private with their care team.
