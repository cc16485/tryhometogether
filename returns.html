Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

We want HomeTogether to feel right for your family. If it isn't, returning it is simple.

30-day risk-free trial. Try HomeTogether for 30 days from the date your device ships. If it's not right, contact us within that window for a full refund of your device and subscription charges — no restocking fees.

How to start a return

Email support@tryhometogether.com or call (417) 234-8494. We'll confirm your return, send simple instructions, and provide a return authorization for the equipment.

What to send back

Please return the Wellness Hub and included accessories (remote, cables, and any sensors or connected devices provided) in good condition. Normal use is fine — we just ask that the equipment isn't damaged beyond ordinary wear.

Refunds

Once we receive and check your return, we'll issue your refund to the original payment method, typically within 5–10 business days. Your bank may take a few extra days to post it. We'll confirm by email when it's done.

Shipping

Shipping to you is free. For trial returns, we'll provide return instructions when you contact us. Please keep your tracking information until your refund is confirmed.

After the trial — cancellations

After the 30-day trial, you can cancel your subscription anytime by contacting us. Cancellation stops future billing; charges already incurred for the current period are generally non-refundable. When you cancel, we'll arrange the return of the Wellness Hub and accessories; equipment not returned may be charged at its replacement value, as described in our Terms of Service.

Defective or damaged equipment

If your device arrives damaged or stops working through no fault of your own, contact us and we'll replace it at no charge.
