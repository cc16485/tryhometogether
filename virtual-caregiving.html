What it is

Caregiving, without anyone leaving home.

Virtual caregiving is a scheduled visit from a trained Caring Companions caregiver that happens over the TV your loved one already uses. At the same times each weekday, a friendly face appears on their screen, no smartphone, no app for them to open, nothing to figure out. They just see someone who cares, and say hello.

On the TV they know

The visit appears right on their television. No new device to learn, and nothing for them to set up or press through.

The same caring face

Weekday visits mean the same caregiver comes at the same times, so real trust and friendship have room to grow.

You stay in control

You choose how visits connect, answer automatically or one button on the remote, and see everything in your family portal.

Simple for them. Reassuring for you.

There is nothing for your loved one to do but be there. Here is what happens each visit, from the moment it begins.

1

We check in, gently

Before the visit even connects, we can see whether there's been recent movement in the home, a quiet signal that all is well.

2

No app. No passwords. No stress.

Everything technical happens on our side. Your loved one never installs anything, never signs in, and never has to remember how it works. When it's time, the visit is simply there, and a warm voice says good morning.

Questions

Virtual caregiving, answered.

Do I need the HomeTogether device to add virtual caregiving?

Yes. Virtual caregiving happens over the HomeTogether device on your loved one's TV, so the device comes first. Many families start with the device for family video calls and wellness, then add caregiver visits when they want an extra set of eyes.

Who are the caregivers?

Trained, background-checked caregivers from Caring Companions, the family-owned home-care agency behind HomeTogether. Weekday scheduling means your loved one tends to see the same familiar face at the same times.

Why only Monday through Friday?

Weekday visits let the same caregiver come at the same times every day, which is how real trust grows. Weekends stay family time, and your family video calling still works 24/7, every day of the week.

Does my loved one have to do anything to answer?

No app, no passwords, nothing to install. You choose how visits connect: they can answer automatically, or press one button on the simple remote. Everything technical happens on our side.

Can I change or cancel the visit plan?

Anytime. Adjust what happens on each visit, add or drop a second visit a day, or pause caregiving altogether while keeping the device. There's no long-term commitment.
